# Source-Capture Checklist

Require:
* discovery lead
* canonical authoritative source
* owner or organization
* exact artifact path
* immutable commit, tag, release, or version
* retrieval date
* raw content hash where practical
* mutable versus immutable URL distinction
* source confidence
* verified fact versus inference versus unknown
* upstream history where relevant

Clarify:
* Reddit, X/Twitter, Instagram, Telegram, Stack Overflow, YouTube, newsletters, catalogs, and awesome lists are discovery leads only.
* Tier 3 sources cannot establish authorship, license, version, safety, or redistribution rights.
